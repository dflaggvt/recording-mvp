## What MemoryStream Does

### Captures Your Soundscape

Enable Continuous Memory once and forget about it. MemoryStream records in the background -- at home, at work, in the car, at the store. It knows when you're speaking and when it's just ambient sound. It tags every moment with where you are and who's there. You live your life; it handles the rest.

### Understands What Was Said

Two transcription engines work in tandem -- one for speed, one for accuracy. Speaker identification tells you who said what. Every word is indexed and searchable, but you never see any of this happening. It's the invisible infrastructure that makes your audio memories navigable.

### Remembers What Matters

Ask your memory anything. "What did Sarah say about the house?" "When is the dentist appointment?" "What was the name of that restaurant?" MemoryStream doesn't just find keywords -- it understands meaning and gives you a direct, natural answer drawn from your actual conversations.

### Keeps You Honest

MemoryStream quietly extracts factual claims from your conversations -- things you stated, preferences you expressed, plans you made -- and compares them over time. If you said one thing last week and something different today, it notices. Not to judge. Just to remember accurately. Your memory, being honest with you.

### Watches Out for You

Proactive insights surface without you asking. Commitments you made that might slip through the cracks. Preferences someone mentioned in passing. A kind thing someone said that's worth remembering. A deadline that's approaching. An inconsistency worth knowing about. These appear gently on your screen -- dismiss them when you're done.

### Recaps Your Day

Open MemoryStream in the evening and see a warm, narrative summary of your day. Where you went, who you spoke with, what was discussed, what was decided. Conversations laid out on a timeline, color-coded by place. Commitments you can check off. It's your day, remembered.

### Knows Your Places

MemoryStream learns where you go. It auto-detects your home, remembers your favorite spots, and uses location to make everything richer -- from tagging conversations with place names to reminding you about a commitment when you arrive at the grocery store.

## How It Works

**1. Enable**
Plug in a USB microphone for the best audio quality, or use your phone's built-in mic. During setup, flip the Continuous Memory toggle. That's it. MemoryStream runs quietly in the background from now on.

**2. Live**
Go about your day. MemoryStream captures everything -- conversations, ambient moments, silences. It transcribes in real time, identifies speakers, extracts what was said, learns your places, and detects what matters. You never interact with it while it's working.

**3. Explore**
When you want to remember something, open the app. Ask a question. Scroll through your timeline -- days of your life, color-coded by place, with a play button on every moment. Tap a chunk and hear it in the persistent audio player. Check your commitments. Read the narrative of your day. The intelligence helps you find what you're looking for; the audio lets you relive it.

## What Is Built Today

MemoryStream's intelligence layer is mature:

- **Continuous recording** with USB microphone support
- **Dual-engine transcription** (real-time Deepgram Nova-3 + OpenAI Whisper post-processing)
- **On-device speaker identification** using neural voiceprints (no cloud processing)
- **Semantic search** with AI-synthesized natural-language answers
- **Factual claim extraction** -- what you stated, promised, and preferred, tracked over time
- **Consistency checking** -- detects contradictions across conversations, cross-references location claims
- **8 types of proactive insights** -- commitments, follow-ups, friction, preferences, positives, unfinished topics, time-sensitive items, and inconsistencies
- **Commitment tracking** with location-triggered geofenced reminders
- **Daily narrative summaries** -- rich, second-person recaps of each day
- **Place learning** -- auto-detects home, tracks locations, enriches memories with place names

The experience layer is taking shape:

- **Ask screen** -- search your memory with natural language, see insight cards, access your day review
- **Timeline** -- a scrollable, infinite-scroll audio timeline organized by day, color-coded by place, with play buttons on every chunk. The audio equivalent of a camera roll.
- **Audio player** -- a persistent mini player that follows you across screens, and a full-screen player with seek, skip, and speaker-labeled transcripts
- **Day Review** -- narrative summary, timeline strip, commitments, inconsistencies, transcript playback
- **Commitments** -- a checklist of things you said you'd do, with overdue highlighting
- **People** -- enroll speakers with a voice sample, manage voice profiles

## Privacy & Trust

Privacy is the foundation, not a feature.

- **Local storage only.** Every recording, transcript, speaker profile, insight, and claim is stored on your device. Nothing is uploaded to a cloud server.
- **No accounts.** No sign-up, no login, no profile.
- **No analytics.** No usage data, no telemetry, no behavioral tracking.
- **Transient processing.** Audio and text are sent to AI services for processing and returned. They are not retained.
- **Your API keys.** You provide your own Deepgram and OpenAI API keys, giving you full control over the services your data touches.
- **On-device voice identification.** Voiceprints are computed and stored entirely on your phone. They never leave the device.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long can I record?**
MemoryStream is designed for continuous recording. With a wake lock and battery optimization exemption, it records for up to 8 hours per session. Audio storage is efficient -- roughly 29 MB per hour -- so even a full day of recordings takes up less space than a few photos.

**Does it work without the USB microphone?**
Yes. The built-in mic works fine. A USB microphone improves quality in noisy environments or when the phone is in your pocket.

**How accurate is the transcription?**
A two-stage system: Deepgram Nova-3 for real-time results, OpenAI Whisper for post-processing accuracy. The combination delivers reliable transcripts for clear speech in most environments.

**Can it tell who is speaking?**
Yes. Enroll speakers with a short voice sample, and MemoryStream identifies them automatically using on-device neural speaker identification. It also detects unknown voices and prompts you to enroll them.

**What's "claim extraction"?**
MemoryStream identifies specific things people say -- facts stated, preferences expressed, plans made -- and stores them as structured claims. These are used to check for consistency over time and to enrich your insights.

**Is my data private?**
Completely. All data stays on your device. Audio is sent to transcription and AI services for processing but is not retained. There are no cloud backups, no user accounts, and no data sharing.

**What happens if I lose my phone?**
All data is local, so losing your phone means losing your recordings. Regular device backups are recommended. Cloud sync is on the roadmap.

**Does it drain my battery?**
Recording uses a wake lock, which consumes battery. Expect 3-5 hours of continuous recording on a charge, depending on your device. Connecting to a charger during long sessions is recommended.

**What languages does it support?**
The current version is optimized for English. Multilingual support is planned.

**How much does it cost?**
MemoryStream is free. It uses Deepgram and OpenAI APIs that have their own pricing. For moderate daily use (1-2 hours of recording), expect a few dollars per month. Both services offer free tiers.
